`astra-emu-fvp` is an MPL-2.0 adaptation derived from behavioral and source study of
[`rfvp`](https://github.com/xmoezzz/rfvp) revision
`3b5ea6c96a925c12f95aef8554905e8fecbc77c3` (the `0.5.0` tag), also licensed under MPL-2.0.

The AstraEMU adaptation replaces rfvp's window, renderer, audio, filesystem and product
shell with Astra provider contracts. Modified source remains available in this crate.
The modification inventory is maintained in `MODIFICATIONS.md` and must ship with the
corresponding covered-source archive or source offer.
